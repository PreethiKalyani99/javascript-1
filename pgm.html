  1. Paragraphs can begin with an indentation (about five spaces), or by missing a line out, and then starting again. This makes it easier to see when one paragraph ends and another begins.
  2. Paragraphs may signal when the writer changes topics. Each paragraph may have a number of sentences,depending on the topic. A pilcrow mark (¶) is sometimes used to show where a paragraph begins.
